# Equations

## Aspect Ratio
$$
\begin{align*}
\frac{16}{9} &= \frac{800}{h} \\
h &= \frac{800}{\left( \frac{16}{9} \right)} \\
h &= 800 \times \frac{9}{16} \\
h &= 450
\end{align*}
$$
